More about:

HIP or Hipparcos Catalogue was the result of the critical comparison and merging of the two (NDAC and FAST consortia) analyses, and contains 118,218 entries (stars or multiple stars), corresponding to an average of some three stars per square degree over the entire sky.

With the respective HIP position you can check properly each stars/planet that we are talking about, also to get a better enphasis I converted the order to TENIS - POLAR code. If you already read Pedro Bandeira books you know what I mean. Enjoy :3.
